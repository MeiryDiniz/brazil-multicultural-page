Festivals & Culture

Brazil celebrates Carnival with samba music, colourful costumes, and big parades. In June, Festa Junina features bonfires, folk dancing, and traditional foods. Capoeira is a unique martial art mixed with music and dance.

Flavours of Brazil

  • 🍛 Feijoada – a stew with beans and meat
  • 🧀 Pão de Queijo – cheesy bread balls
  • 🍫 Brigadeiro – chocolate truffles
  • 🍇 Açaí Bowl – frozen Amazonian fruit treat

Brazilian Nature

Brazil is home to the Amazon Rainforest and River, pink dolphins, jaguars, toucans, and sloths. You can also visit the stunning Iguaçu Falls in Paraná.

My City: Maringá

We came from Southern Brazil

  • 📍 Maringá, Paraná: A beautiful city in the state of Paraná, in the South of Brazil.
  • 🌳 Known for its green spaces, tree-lined streets, and modern urban design.
  • 🏙️ One of the best cities to live in Brazil.
  • 🛐 Famous landmark: Catedral Basílica Menor Nossa Senhora da Glória (Cathedral of Maringá)
  • 🎓 Home to many universities and cultural events.

Brazil vs Ireland

  • 🌍 Geography: Brazil is the 5th largest country in the world X Ireland is a small island in Europe, known for green hills.
  • 🌦️ Weather: Brazil has tropical weather; it's hot in most places X Ireland has a mild, rainy climate – four seasons in one day!
  • 🗣️ Language: Brazil speaks Portuguese X Ireland speaks English and Irish (Gaeilge).
  • 🎉 Festivals: Brazil: Carnival with parades and samba X Ireland: St. Patrick’s Day – green clothes and music!
  • 🎵 Music & Dance: Brazil: Samba and Bossa Nova X Ireland: Irish music and dancing (like Riverdance).
  • 🍲 Food: Brazil: Feijoada, Cheese Bread, Brigadeiro X Ireland: Irish stew, Soda bread, Boxty.
  • ⚽ Sports: Brazil: Football (soccer) – World Cup champions! X Ireland: Gaelic football, hurling, rugby.
  • 🧒 School: Brazil: Morning or afternoon shifts X Ireland: School from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m.
  • 📏 Geography Fun Fact: Ireland: ~70,000 km². São Paulo state: ~248,000 km² (3.5x bigger!) X São Paulo city has more people than all of Ireland!
