Welcome to Brazil!

Explore Brazil’s culture, nature, food, and more. Learn about my hometown Maringá!

Culture & Festivals

Brazil is famous for its Carnival celebration with parades, samba music, and colorful costumes.

Brazilian Food

Try Feijoada, a black bean and meat stew, or Brigadeiros, our chocolate sweets.

Nature & Animals

The Amazon Rainforest is the world’s largest. Brazil is home to toucans, jaguars, and sloths.

My City: Maringá

Maringá is in southern Brazil. It’s known for green parks, modern architecture, and the beautiful cathedral.
