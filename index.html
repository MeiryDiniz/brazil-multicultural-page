Welcome to Brazil!

Brazil is the largest country in South America. Its capital is Brasília. People speak Portuguese, and it’s famous for football, the Amazon, Christ the Redeemer, and Carnival!

Festivals & Culture

Brazil celebrates Carnival with samba music, colourful costumes, and big parades. In June, Festa Junina features bonfires, folk dancing, and traditional foods. Capoeira is a unique martial art mixed with music and dance.

Flavours of Brazil

  • 🍛 Feijoada – a stew with beans and meat
  • 🧀 Pão de Queijo – cheesy bread balls
  • 🍫 Brigadeiro – chocolate truffles
  • 🍇 Açaí Bowl – frozen Amazonian fruit treat

Brazilian Nature

Brazil is home to the Amazon Rainforest and River, pink dolphins, jaguars, toucans, and sloths. You can also visit the stunning Iguaçu Falls in Paraná.

Kids Like You in Brazil

Kids in Brazil love football and dancing. School often ends around 1 p.m. Learn some Portuguese:

  • Olá = Hello
  • Tudo bem? = How are you?
  • Tchau = Bye
  • Obrigado/a = Thank you

My City: Maringá

📍 Maringá is a beautiful city in Paraná, southern Brazil. It has tree-lined streets, modern design, and is one of the best cities to live in Brazil. It’s known for the Cathedral of Maringá and many parks like Parque do Ingá and Parque do Japão.
